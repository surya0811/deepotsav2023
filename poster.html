We Cordially Invite

Deepotsav, our grand Diwali festival celebration, is a time-honored tradition at Sri Chandrasekharendra Saraswathi Viswa Mahavidyalaya (SCSVMV). It's a celebration that illuminates our campus with joy, culture, and vibrant colors.

What is Deepotsav?

Deepotsav, meaning the "Festival of Lights," is our way of coming together as a community to celebrate the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignoranceï¿½core themes of the Diwali festival. It's a time when our entire college campus is adorned with a mesmerizing display of lamps, lanterns, and colorful rangoli patterns.

What to Expect?

Deepotsav is not just a festival; it's a reflection of our college's values and commitment to fostering unity, cultural diversity, and a sense of belonging. It's a time when our students, faculty, staff, and the local community come together to strengthen bonds and celebrate the rich tapestry of Indian culture.

Join Us at #Deepotsav

We invite you to be a part of this joyous occasion. Whether you're a student, alumni, staff, or a visitor from the community, Deepotsav welcomes all to celebrate the festival of lights with us. Join us for a memorable experience filled with laughter, music, dance, and the warmth of togetherness.

What to Expect?

At Deepotsav, you can expect a spectacular array of activities and events that cater to every age group and interest. From traditional dance performances and music recitals to enlightening talks and workshops, there's something for everyone. Be prepared to savor mouthwatering Indian cuisine, including sweets and snacks that are a hallmark of Diwali celebrations.


Come, let's illuminate our hearts and minds together at Deepotsav@2k23!
